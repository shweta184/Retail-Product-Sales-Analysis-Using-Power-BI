# 📊 Business Performance & Profitability Analytics – Power BI

## ⚙️ Tools Used
Power BI | DAX | Power Query | Excel  

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes overall business performance and profitability using a multi-page Power BI dashboard.

The dashboard evaluates **sales trends, product performance, regional profitability, customer contribution, and operational efficiency** to identify revenue drivers and margin risks.

---

## 🧠 Business Objective

- Monitor revenue and profit performance  
- Identify high-revenue but low-margin products  
- Detect loss-making regions and customers  
- Analyze return rate and shipping impact on profitability  
- Improve operational efficiency  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Identified **high-revenue but low-margin products**, highlighting pricing or cost optimization opportunities.  
- Detected **loss-making regions and customers**, indicating the need for strategic review.  
- Return rate analysis revealed its direct impact on overall profitability.  
- Shipping performance evaluation uncovered cost inefficiencies affecting margins.  
- Customer contribution analysis helped identify top revenue drivers and high-risk accounts.  

---

## 🗂️ Data Model & Approach
 
- Created DAX measures for Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin %, and Return Rate  
- Performed contribution and segmentation analysis  

---

## 🎯 Recommendations

- Reassess pricing or cost structure for low-margin high-volume products  
- Optimize operations in loss-making regions  
- Improve return management processes  
- Focus retention strategies on high-value customers
